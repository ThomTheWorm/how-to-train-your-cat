Fact 1

Cats are one of the most popular pets in the world.



Fact 2

They have been domesticated for over 9,000 years.



Fact 3

Cats have a natural instinct to hunt, even when domesticated.



Fact 4

They have a flexible spine and powerful legs that allow them to jump great distances.



Fact 5

Cats have excellent vision, especially in low light conditions.



Fact 6

Their sense of smell is also highly developed.



Fact 7

Cats groom themselves frequently to keep clean and maintain their fur.



Fact 8

They are known for their independence and solitary nature.



Fact 9

Cats communicate through body language, vocalizations, and scent marking.



Fact 10

Some breeds of cats can live over 20 years.
